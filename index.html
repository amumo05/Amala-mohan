I'm studying Computer Science and Mathematics, with a particular pull toward front-end development and UI/UX design. I love the challenge of structuring information so it feels effortless — turning complexity into something anyone can understand and enjoy using.

I deliberately explore different areas of computer science to build breadth alongside depth — the most interesting problems usually live at the edges of disciplines. Hackathons are my favourite environment for this: tight timelines, high stakes, and a team to learn from.
